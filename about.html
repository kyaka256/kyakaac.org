About Us

Welcome to KYAKA AFRICAN CULTURE ORGANISATION

we are an organisation that helps to advance culture by many ways like keeping items that are significant to african cultures especailly uganda's popular cultures.

and holding exhibitions of the items to the public. also provide help for needy people mostly orphans,widows,eldery people and crippled ones, WE also establish operating groups where poor and widow parents can take their children to school

To collect, preserve, and exhibit items of historical significance of kingdoms, traditional sites for the benefit of the community. To support the restoration and maintenance of buildings of historical significance to Kingdoms and traditional sites for the beneft of community. To foster public awareness of and interest of the traditional cultures. To source scholarships for young people from disadvantaged backgrounds to attend advanced studies in academics, skilling and training in traditional and modern dances. To advance education by providing life skills training to young people and disabled fromn disadvantaged backgrounds. To fund workshops so that young people are able to share their knowledge of culture with the community. To advance education and relieve poverty by providing scholarships for young people who are financially disadvantaged to study at school or educational institutions. To enhance the natural environment of Uganda by promoting sustainability and sustainable development and use of resources. To enhance the natural environment of Uganda by promnoting the planting of indigenous Ugandan trees and plant species in appropriate locations. To provide a forum for all people engaged in cultural industry to discuss best practices and enhancing the future of the industry.
